About Michael Park School

Step into our unique school village, nestled within the big city

Michael Park School is the only Steiner school in central Auckland, and we take care of the learning journey right through from Kindergarten to High School.

Our students are encouraged to “Relate, Create and Inspire” – building healthy relationships with those around them, enjoying new projects, and finding the inspiration to seek out their unique purpose in the world and pursue it with courage.

We invite you to explore our site and enjoy getting to know Michael Park School. Read Our Story, find out more about Kindergarten, Junior School, Middle School, and High School, meet Our People and learn how to get involved in Our Community.
